PROJECT GLAN

"A COLLABORATION BETWEEN FRIENDS" 2017

This is the first Full stack project that i made with 2 other friends, we used Angular.js in combination with expres.js and a lot more packages, this project took us more than 6 months to complete and has a lot of features, and is still being used every year at our lanparty. Some of the features: register and ticketing system with payment methods, live interaction screen for competitions that are being played at the lanparty, Tournament registration pages, Registry tool for our catering (cash register system), extended administration dashboard.
